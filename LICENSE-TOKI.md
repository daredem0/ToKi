Commercial use is allowed under this license.

You do not need a separate written agreement for normal commercial releases, as long as you follow the rules below.

6. Royalty rules

You pay no royalty on the first €10,000 of gross revenue per product.

After that, royalties are calculated in tiers:

- 0% on the first €10,000
- 2% on revenue above €10,000 up to €50,000
- 3% on revenue above €50,000 up to €100,000
- 5% on revenue above €100,000

This is progressive.

That means each rate only applies to the portion of revenue in that range.

Example:
If your game makes €120,000 gross revenue, the royalty is:

- 0% on the first €10,000
- 2% on the next €40,000
- 3% on the next €50,000
- 5% on the final €20,000

7. What “gross revenue” means

“Gross revenue” means money earned from a product made with ToKi.

This includes:

- game sales
- DLC and expansions
- in-app purchases
- subscriptions tied to the product
- crowdfunding tied to the product
- ad revenue
- publisher advances for the product
- bundle revenue reasonably attributable to the product
- platform or streaming payouts tied to the product

You should define more detailed accounting rules in the final legal version, especially for:

- taxes
- platform fees

- revenue splits

- report your revenue every calendar quarter
- pay any royalty due within 30 days after the end of that quarter
